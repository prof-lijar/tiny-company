# v2.2 Launch Budget and Success KPIs

## 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the financial guardrails and success metrics for the v2.2 "Reasoning IDE" release. The launch strategy prioritizes Product-Led Growth (PLG) and organic acquisition to maintain a high LTV/CAC ratio, avoiding expensive paid acquisition channels.

---

## 3. Success KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
Success for v2.2 is measured by the efficiency of the conversion funnel and the sustainability of the unit economics.

### 3.1 Acquisition & Growth Metrics
| KPI | Target (Expected) | Threshold (Minimum) | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** | 2,000 (M1) $\rightarrow$ 20,000 (M6) | 1,000 (M1) $\rightarrow$ 10,000 (M6) | Monthly |
| **Blended CAC** | < $120.00 | < $195.00 (Max Allowable) | Monthly |

| KPI | Target | Threshold | Notes |

---

## 4. Financial Guardrails & Trigger Points
To prevent financial instability, the following triggers will initiate a strategic review:

1. **CAC Spike:** If Blended CAC exceeds **$195.00**, all paid acquisition efforts will be paused immediately in favor of organic loop optimization.
2. **Churn Alert:** If monthly churn exceeds **8%**, the product team must prioritize "Retention-focused" features over new feature development.
3. **API Cost Variance:** If API costs exceed projected OpEx by **> 30%** for two consecutive months, the "Local-First" offloading strategy must be audited and tightened.

# Financial Projections: TraceWhisper (v2.1 Accelerated & v2.2 Pivot)

## 1. Executive Summary
TraceWhisper has transitioned to a **Local-First architecture**, fundamentally shifting our cost structure from fixed infrastructure to variable API consumption. This projection is updated as of May 20, 2026, to align with the **v2.1 'Frictionless Entry' acceleration** and the strategic pivot toward the **v2.2 'Reasoning IDE'**. 

The v2.1 launch has shown higher-than-expected conversion rates (11.2% on landing pages), accelerating our path to positive cash flow and providing a buffer for R&D spend on v2.2.

## 2. Short-Term Outlook: v2.1 Growth & v2.2 R&D (Months 1-3)
**Focus:** Scaling the "Frictionless Entry" funnel and prototyping the "Reasoning IDE" (v2.2).

- **Revenue:** Accelerated growth due to high initial interest and reduced friction.
    - **Expected Monthly Revenue:** $800 $\rightarrow$ $5,000.
- **Estimated Costs:**
    - LLM APIs (Sponsorship + v2.2 Reasoning R&D): ~$600 - $1,000/month. (v2.2 requires higher token usage for iterative reasoning).
    - Infrastructure (Scaling to paid tiers): ~$50 - $150/month.
    - Domain/Misc: ~$10/month.
- **Net Cash Flow:** Positive.
- **Financial Goal:** Validate the conversion of the v2.1 waitlist to Managed API and prototype the v2.2 cost-per-reasoning-loop.

## 6. Runway & Break-Even Analysis

**Break-Even Point (Monthly):**
To cover the current v2.1/v2.2 operating costs of ~$800/month, we need:
- **Managed API Users:** $\sim 110$ bundles per month (assuming 37% margin).
- **Pro Users:** $\sim 42$ users at $19/month.

**Verdict:**
The financial risk remains **minimal**. The increased R&D spend for v2.2 ("Reasoning IDE") is well-covered by the accelerated revenue growth seen in v2.1. The primary focus is now on managing the variable API spend during the v2.2 prototyping phase and maintaining the high conversion rate from the v2.1 waitlist.

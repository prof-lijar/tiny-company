# GTM Strategy: 2026 TOPIK Reform Readiness

## 1. Strategic Objective
Position **TOPIK Flow** as the definitive, most up-to-date preparation platform for the 2026 TOPIK overhaul. We aim to capture the "anxiety market"—learners who are concerned that their existing study materials are obsolete due to the 2026 changes.

## 2. Target Audience
- **The Anxious Intermediate:** TOPIK II learners who have been studying with old materials and fear the new 2026 standards.
- **The High-Stakes Candidate:** University students and visa applicants (e.g., E-7-4) who cannot afford to fail and need the most accurate simulation.
- **The Modern Self-Studier:** Tech-savvy learners who prefer web-based, AI-powered tools over fragmented PDFs and mobile apps.

## 3. Value Proposition
**"The only platform fully aligned with the 2026 TOPIK overhaul."**
We don't just provide questions; we provide the *new standard* of preparation, including AI-driven writing feedback and modernized listening speeds.

## 4. Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: The "What's New" Series (Educational)
*Goal: Establish authority by simplifying the complex 2026 reform.*
- **Blog/Social Posts:** "5 Things You Must Know About the 2026 TOPIK Reform."
- **Comparison Guides:** "Old TOPIK vs. 2026 TOPIK: What actually changes for you?"
- **Checklists:** "Is your current study plan 2026-ready?"

### Pillar 2: The "New Standard" Features (Product)
*Goal: Show, don't just tell, how our features solve the new challenges.*
- **1.1x Speed Listening:** Focus on the shift toward more natural, faster conversational speeds. "Don't be surprised on exam day—train at 1.1x speed."
- **Anti-Template Writing:** Focus on the AI's ability to detect and penalize "canned" responses. "Stop memorizing templates. Start writing naturally with AI feedback."
- **IBT Simulation:** Emphasize the web-first experience for those transitioning to digital testing.

### Pillar 3: The "Confidence" Arc (Psychological)
*Goal: Move users from fear to confidence via social proof.*
- **Hypothetical Success Stories:** "How Min-jun shifted from old textbooks to TOPIK Flow and scored Level 6 under the new guidelines."
- **User Milestones:** Highlighting users who have completed the "2026 Readiness Path."

| Channel | Strategy | Key Format |

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Conversion Rate:** % of landing page visitors signing up for the "2026 Readiness" plan.
- **Organic Traffic:** Growth in search queries related to "2026 TOPIK".
- **User Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Efficiency of organic channels vs. potential paid ads.

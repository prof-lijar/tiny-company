# Launch Content & Messaging: TraceWhisper

## 1. Key Messaging & Positioning

### Positioning Statement
TraceWhisper is the narrative synthesis layer for AI agents. While traditional observability tools show you *what* happened (spans, latency, tokens), TraceWhisper tells you *why* it happened. It bridges the "Semantic Gap" between raw JSON traces and human understanding.

### Core Value Propositions
- **Stop Scrolling, Start Reading:** Convert thousands of lines of logs into a concise, narrative Execution Report.
- **Debug the "Why", Not the "What":** Instantly identify where an agent's reasoning diverged from the goal.
- **Audit-Ready Transparency:** Make autonomous agent behavior understandable for non-technical stakeholders and compliance officers.
- **90% Cognitive Reduction:** Eliminate the mental overhead of parsing recursive tool-call loops.

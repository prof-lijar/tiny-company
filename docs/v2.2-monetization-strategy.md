# v2.2 Monetization & Pricing Strategy: The Reasoning IDE

## 1. Executive Summary
With the transition of TraceWhisper v2.2 from a diagnostic tool to a "Reasoning IDE," we are moving from a simple utility model to a high-value productivity platform. The v2.2 release introduces tools that not only detect errors but actively correct them and prevent regressions in production. 

This strategy introduces a tiered pricing model designed to capture value from three distinct segments: the Hobbyist (Free), the Professional Developer (Pro), and the Engineering Organization (Enterprise).

## 3. Proposed Pricing Model

### A. Free Tier: "The Explorer"
*Designed for individual developers and hobbyists to get started.*
- **Price:** $0 / month (BYOK - Bring Your Own Key)
- **Value Prop:** "See how your agent thinks."
- **Constraint:** Detection only. No automated fixes or team features.

### B. Pro Tier: "The Optimizer"
*Designed for professional AI engineers optimizing complex agentic workflows.*
- **Price:** $39 / month / user
- **Value Prop:** "Collapse the iteration loop. Fix reasoning errors in seconds, not hours."
- **Core Value:** Access to the **Correction Suite** (Fix-It, A/B Testing, Break-points).
- **Billing:** Monthly or Annual ($390/year - 2 months free).

### C. Enterprise Tier: "The Governor"
*Designed for organizations deploying agents to production where reliability is non-negotiable.*
- **Price:** $149 / month / user (Minimum 5 seats)
- **Value Prop:** "Zero-regression AI deployments. Hard gates for cognitive quality."
- **Core Value:** **CI/CD Guardrails** and **Team Logic Sharing**.
- **Billing:** Annual contracts only.

## 5. Strategic Justification
1. **Value-Based Pricing:** We are not pricing based on "tokens" or "reports," but on the **time saved** by the developer. A "Fix-It" button that saves 2 hours of prompt engineering is worth significantly more than a monthly subscription.
2. **The "Hard Gate" Moat:** By placing CI/CD Guardrails in the Enterprise tier, we move from being a "nice-to-have" tool to a "mandatory" part of the production pipeline (similar to Snyk or SonarQube for code).
3. **Low Overhead:** Due to the Local-First architecture, the marginal cost of adding a Pro/Enterprise user is extremely low, ensuring high gross margins.

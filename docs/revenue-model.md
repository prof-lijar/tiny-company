# Revenue Model: TOPIK Learning Assistant

This document outlines the monetization strategy, pricing tiers, and revenue projections for the TOPIK Learning Assistant.

## 1. Pricing Strategy: Freemium Model

The app follows a freemium model designed to acquire a large user base through free value and convert power users into paid subscribers.

### Free Tier (The "Hook")
- **Target**: Casual learners and beginners.
- **Features**: 
    - Limited daily vocabulary and grammar practice (e.g., 10 items/day).
    - Access to basic mock test samples.
    - Basic progress tracking.
- **Goal**: User acquisition and habit formation.

### Pro Tier (The "Value")
- **Price**: $12/month (Suggested range: $8 - $15)
- **Annual Plan**: $110/year (~$9.16/month, ~24% discount)
- **Features**:
    - Unlimited daily practice.
    - **AI Writing Feedback**: Unlimited automated evaluations of TOPIK writing tasks.
    - Full suite of mock tests with detailed explanations.
    - Spaced Repetition System (SRS) for vocabulary.
    - Advanced analytics and proficiency forecasting.
- **Goal**: High LTV (Lifetime Value) and sustainable revenue.

## 3. Revenue Projections

### Assumptions
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: 200,000+ annual test takers.
- **Conversion Rate**: 5% (Conservative) $\rightarrow$ 10% (Optimistic).
- **Churn Rate**: 10% monthly.
- **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)**: $11/month (weighted average of monthly/annual).

## 4. Break-Even Analysis

### Monthly Fixed Costs (at 1k users)
- Infrastructure (Vercel, Supabase, Domain, etc.): ~$50 - $100 / month.
- AI Costs (Variable): ~$0.30 per active Pro user / month.

### Break-Even Point
- **Contribution Margin per User**: $11 (Revenue) - $0.30 (AI Cost) = $10.70.
- **Fixed Costs**: ~$100.
- **Break-Even Users**: $100 / $10.70 $\approx$ **10 Paid Subscribers**.

The project becomes cash-flow positive almost immediately upon acquiring a small number of paying users, provided marketing costs are kept low (organic growth).

## 5. Growth Levers
- **Referral Program**: "Refer a friend, get 1 month of Pro free" to lower CAC.
- **Institutional Partnerships**: B2B licensing for Korean language schools.
- **Seasonal Spikes**: Aggressive marketing 2 months prior to official TOPIK exam dates.

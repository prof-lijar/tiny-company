## 2. Detailed Tier Breakdown

### 🟢 Enterprise Team
*The "Power User" team upgrade.*
- **Price:** $249 per user / month.
- **Included Features:**
    - All v2.3 Enterprise features.
    - Team-Shared Vaults (shared prompts/patterns).
    - Reasoning Peer Review (GitHub/GitLab integration).
    - Basic Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
- **Limits:** No hard limits on usage, subject to fair-use policy.

### 🔵 Enterprise Organization
*The "Governance" layer for the whole company.*
- **Base Fee:** $2,500 / month.
- **Included Seats:** Up to 50 users.
- **Metered Usage Fees:**
    - **Golden Path Definition:** $10 per path.
    - **APO (Autonomous Prompt Optimization):** $50 per 1,000 runs.
- **Included Features:**
    - Everything in Enterprise Team.
    - Organizational Golden Paths (Standardized reasoning).
    - Compliance Scanning (Automated audit of all agent prompts).
    - Policy-Based Guardrails (Prevent banned reasoning patterns).
    - Centralized Governance Dashboard.

### 🔴 Enterprise Custom
*The "Sovereign" deployment for high-compliance.*
- **Price:** Custom Annual Contract (Estimated $50k - $250k / year).
- **Included Features:**
    - Everything in Enterprise Organization.
    - **Data Residency:** Choice of region or On-Prem/VPC deployment.
    - **Dedicated Support:** 24/7 SLA and Dedicated Account Manager.
    - **Custom IdP:** Advanced SAML/OIDC/Active Directory integration.
    - **Unlimited Usage:** Capped by fair-use, no per-run fees.
- **Governance:** Full audit logs and regulatory alignment support.

## 3. Migration Path (v2.3 $\rightarrow$ v2.4)
- **Existing Enterprise Users:** Will be offered a 3-month grace period to migrate to the **Enterprise Team** tier at a discounted rate of $219/mo before the $249/mo price takes effect.
- **Upsell Path:** Enterprise Team clients showing high usage of shared patterns will be targeted for the **Enterprise Organization** tier.

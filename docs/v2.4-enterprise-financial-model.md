# Financial Model: v2.4 Enterprise Scale-Up

## 1. Executive Summary
With the transition to v2.4, TraceWhisper moves from a productivity tool to an **Enterprise Reasoning Governance Platform**. The financial objective is to shift from a purely per-seat model to a value-based model that captures the organizational utility of "Reasoning Governance" and "Institutional Knowledge."

The core value driver in v2.4 is the **Centralized Knowledge Graph of Agent Intelligence**, which creates high switching costs and justifies a significant price premium over individual Pro seats.

---

## 2. Enterprise Pricing Tiers
To accommodate different organizational sizes and needs, we are introducing three distinct Enterprise tiers.

### Tier 1: Enterprise Team (Per-Seat)
*Target: Small to mid-sized AI teams (5-20 engineers).*
- **Price:** **$249 / user / month** (Increase from v2.3 $199)
- **Included:** 
    - All v2.3 Enterprise features.
    - Team-Shared Vaults.
    - Reasoning Peer Review (Git Integration).
    - Basic RBAC.
- **Value Prop:** "Collaborative Intelligence." Stop reinventing the wheel within the team.

### Tier 2: Enterprise Organization (Base + Usage)
*Target: Mid-to-large organizations with multiple AI projects.*
- **Price:** **$2,500 / month base fee** + Usage components.
    - **Usage Fee:** $10 per "Golden Path" defined.
    - **APO Fee:** $50 per 1,000 Autonomous Prompt Optimization (APO) runs.
- **Included:** 
    - Everything in Team Tier.
    - Organizational Golden Paths & Compliance Scanning.
    - Policy-Based Guardrails.
    - Centralized Governance Dashboard.
    - Up to 50 users included in base fee.
- **Value Prop:** "Reasoning Governance." Ensure consistency and safety across all corporate agents.

### Tier 3: Enterprise Custom (Flat-Fee / Annual)
*Target: Fortune 500 / Highly Regulated Industries.*
- **Price:** **Custom Annual Contract** (Estimated $50k - $250k / year)
- **Included:** 
    - Everything in Organization Tier.
    - **Data Residency:** Choice of region/On-prem deployment options.
    - **Dedicated Support:** SLA and dedicated account manager.
    - **Custom IdP Integration:** Advanced SAML/OIDC configurations.
    - Unlimited Golden Paths and APO runs (capped by fair-use).
- **Value Prop:** "Compliance and Sovereignty." Full control over intelligence assets and regulatory alignment.

## 5. Infrastructure Budget Impact (v2.4)

As we move from planning to active implementation (v2.4 Core), we are refining our cost estimates into two phases: **Initial Implementation** (Current) and **Scale-Up** (Post-Launch).

### 5.1 Implementation Costs (Current Phase)
Focuses on establishing the multi-tenant architecture and the initial vault system.
- **Multi-tenancy:** Initial setup of schema-per-tenant on a mid-tier managed DB. (~$100 - $300/mo)
- **Audit Logging:** Initial S3 bucket and indexing for low volume. (~$50 - $100/mo)
- **IdP Integration:** Leveraging free-tier/open-source OIDC providers. (~$0 - $50/mo)
- **APO Compute:** R&D and internal testing tokens. (~$200 - $500/mo)
- **Phase 1 Total Added OpEx:** **$350 - $950 / month**

### 5.2 Scale-Up Costs (Growth Phase)
Costs that scale linearly or in steps with the number of Enterprise clients.
- **Multi-tenancy:** Scaling DB instances to handle high-availability and increased tenant count. (~$500 - $1,500/mo)
- **Audit Logging:** High-volume writes and compliance-grade immutable storage. (~$200 - $500/mo)
- **IdP Integration:** Managed SAML/OIDC services for Enterprise clients. (~$100 - $300/mo)
- **APO Compute:** Heavy production load, offset by per-run fees. (~$1,000 - $5,000/mo)
- **Phase 2 Total Added OpEx:** **$1,800 - $7,300 / month**

### 5.3 Summary of Added Monthly OpEx (v2.4)

## 6. Pricing Validation & Margins
Cross-referencing these costs with `docs/v2.4-pricing-sheet.md`:

- **Enterprise Team ($249/user):** High margin. 10 users ($2,490) cover the entire implementation phase OpEx.
- **Enterprise Org ($2,500 base):** Extremely high margin. One Org client covers the implementation phase OpEx and supports the initial scale-up phase.
- **APO Unit Economics:** 1,000 runs are billed at $50. We target a 60-80% gross margin on these runs, meaning compute cost should stay under $10-$20 per 1,000 runs.
- **Conclusion:** The v2.4 pricing model is robust and provides significant runway to cover the infrastructure expansion.

## 7. Financial Risk Mitigation
- **APO Margin Erosion:** If APO costs spike, we will implement hard token caps per organization and move to a "Credit" system.
- **Custom Tier Over-servicing:** To prevent "Custom" clients from draining engineering resources, we will limit the number of custom feature requests to 2 per quarter.
- **Sales Cycle Lag:** Enterprise sales cycles are longer. We will maintain the Pro tier as our primary cash-flow engine while the Enterprise pipeline matures.

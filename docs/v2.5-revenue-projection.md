# Revenue Projection: v2.5 The Autonomous Bridge

## 1. Executive Summary
This document provides a detailed revenue forecast for the transition from v2.4 (Passive Governance) to v2.5 (Active Optimization). 

The v2.5 financial strategy shifts from a seat-based Enterprise model to a **Platform + Credit** model. This allows us to decouple the cost of platform access (fixed) from the high compute costs of autonomous reasoning optimization (variable).

**Key Goal:** Increase ARPU for Enterprise Organization clients by introducing the "Bridge" as a premium value-add that actively reduces their own LLM costs through optimization.

---

## 2. Revenue Drivers & Assumptions

### 2.1 The "Bridge" Revenue Stack
Revenue for v2.5 is additive to the v2.4 Enterprise baseline.

1. **Bridge Platform Fee (Fixed):** 
   - **$500 / month / organization**
   - This is a surcharge on the v2.4 Enterprise Organization tier.
   - Provides access to the Bridge Dashboard and baseline Drift Detection (1%).

2. **Optimization Credits (Variable):**
   - **$100 per 1,000 Credits**
   - Credits are consumed by high-value autonomous operations:
     - Logic Porter Translation (50 cr)
     - Shadow APO Run (200 cr)
     - High-Fidelity Drift Scanning (10 cr / 100 traces)
     - Auto-SOP Generation (100 cr)

### 2.2 Adoption Assumptions (Conservative Scenario)
- **v2.4 Baseline:** We assume the v2.4 Enterprise rollout is stable with ~50 Organization-tier clients.
- **v2.5 Migration Rate:** 40% of Org clients migrate to the Bridge Platform over the first 6 months (Total: 20 clients).
- **Credit Consumption:** Each Bridge client spends an average of $100 - $250 per month on credits during the first year.

## 5. Margin Analysis (The "Credit" Advantage)

The shift to credits is a strategic move to protect gross margins against LLM price volatility.

- **Fixed Fee Margin:** The $500/mo platform fee has near-zero marginal cost (excluding the 1% sampling), resulting in ~98% margin.
- **Credit Margin:** 
    - **Example: Logic Porter Translation**
        - Cost: $0.50 - $1.00 (LLM API)
        - Revenue: $5.00 (50 credits)
        - **Gross Margin: 80% - 90%**
    - **Example: Shadow APO Run**
        - Cost: $2.00 - $5.00 (LLM API)
        - Revenue: $20.00 (200 credits)
        - **Gross Margin: 75% - 90%**

By pricing credits at a significant premium over the API cost, we create a buffer that allows us to use the most capable (and expensive) frontier models for the "Bridge" logic without eroding our net profit.

## 6. Financial Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Financial Impact | Mitigation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Low Credit Adoption** | Flat revenue growth | Bundle 1,000 free credits with the Platform Fee to seed usage habits. |
| **API Cost Spike** | Margin compression | Implement "Credit Tiers" (Standard vs. Premium) based on the underlying model used. |
| **v2.4 $\rightarrow$ v2.5 Churn** | Loss of baseline MRR | Market v2.5 not as a "new version" but as an "Optimization Upgrade" that saves the client money. |

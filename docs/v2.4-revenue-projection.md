# v2.4 Enterprise Revenue Projection (12-Month Outlook)

## 1. Executive Summary
The transition to v2.4 shifts TraceWhisper from a per-seat utility to an organizational governance platform. This projection assumes a phased rollout starting from the current v2.3 baseline, focusing on migrating existing power users and acquiring new mid-to-large scale organization clients.

---

## 2. Key Assumptions

### Revenue Streams
- **Enterprise Team:** $249/user/mo.
- **Enterprise Org:** $2,500 base/mo + Usage (est. $500/mo avg usage).
- **Enterprise Custom:** $100k average annual contract (recognized monthly as ~$8,333).

### Growth Velocity
- **Migration:** 50% of existing 100 Enterprise users (v2.3) migrate to Team tier by Month 3.
- **Acquisition:**
    - Months 1-3: Focus on migration + 1 Org client.
    - Months 4-6: 2 new Org clients / month.
    - Months 7-12: 1 new Org client / month + 1 Custom client in Month 9.

## 4. Cumulative Revenue & Growth Analysis

- **Estimated Year 1 Total Revenue:** ~$443,000
- **MRR Growth:** From ~$12k to ~$77k (approx 6x growth in 12 months).
- **Concentration Risk:** By Month 12, Enterprise Org and Custom tiers represent ~60% of revenue. This is acceptable given the higher LTV and lower churn of organizational lock-in.

## 5. Sensitivity Analysis

- **Optimistic:** Faster migration (80%) and 2 Custom clients $\rightarrow$ Year 1 Revenue: ~$600k.
- **Conservative:** Slow migration (30%) and only 5 Org clients total $\rightarrow$ Year 1 Revenue: ~$220k.

## 6. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to Track
1. **Migration Rate:** % of v2.3 users moving to v2.4 Team.
2. **Org Expansion:** Average usage revenue per Org client (Target: $500/mo).
3. **CAC Payback:** Time to recover the high Enterprise acquisition cost (Target: < 12 months).

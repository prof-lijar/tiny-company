# v2 Beta Acquisition Plan: The First 100

## 1. Objective
Acquire 100 high-signal beta testers for TraceWhisper v2 to validate the "IDE for Agent Reasoning" value proposition and stress-test the v2 SDK and Live Whisper features.

## 2. Target Audience (The "High-Signal" User)
We aren't looking for general curiosity; we want users who are actively struggling with agent reliability.
- **The Frustrated AI Engineer:** Building complex agents with LangChain/AutoGPT/CrewAI who are tired of reading 10k lines of raw logs.
- **The LLMOps Lead:** Responsible for productionizing agents and needing quantitative proof of prompt improvement.
- **The "Local-First" Enthusiast:** Users running local models who need observability without sending all their traces to a third-party cloud.

## 3. Acquisition Channels & Strategy

### A. The "Warm" Base (Existing v1 Users)
- **Tactic:** Direct email to v1 users.
- **Messaging:** "You've seen how TraceWhisper summarizes the past. Now, see it in real-time. Join the v2 Beta."
- **Goal:** 30 users.

### B. The "Hubs" (X/Twitter & LinkedIn)
- **Tactic:** A "Build-in-Public" thread showcasing a side-by-side comparison of a failing agent vs. an optimized agent using TraceWhisper v2.
- **Messaging:** "Stop the prompt-and-pray cycle. We're opening 100 spots for the IDE for Agent Reasoning."
- **Goal:** 40 users.

### C. The "Deep-Dive" Communities (Reddit r/LocalLLM, r/LangChain, Hacker News)
- **Tactic:** High-value technical posts. Instead of a "pitch," we share a "Lesson Learned" post about the "Semantic Gap" in agent logs, then offer the beta as a solution.
- **Messaging:** "Why traditional logging fails for autonomous agents (and how we're fixing it with v2)."
- **Goal:** 30 users.

## 4. The Conversion Funnel

1. **Discovery:** User sees post/email $\rightarrow$ clicks link.
2. **Intent:** Lands on `v2-landing-page-copy.md` $\rightarrow$ enters email for waitlist.
3. **Confirmation:** Receives "You're on the list" email with a "Fast Track" option (share on X/Twitter to move up).
4. **Legal Gate:** User is invited to Beta $\rightarrow$ must sign the **Beta Testing Agreement (PR #42)**.
5. **Onboarding:** User receives SDK keys and a "Quick Start" guide to their first Live Whisper session.

## 5. Success Metrics
- **Quantity:** 100 signed Beta Agreements.
- **Quality:** $\ge$ 50% of users are implementing the SDK in a non-trivial project.
- **Feedback:** $\ge$ 20 detailed feedback reports on the "The Fixer" (Prompt Surgery) feature.

## 6. Timeline
- **Week 1:** Finalize Legal Agreement & Landing Page.
- **Week 2:** Launch "Warm Base" email & "Build-in-Public" threads.
- **Week 3:** Community outreach (Reddit/HN).
- **Week 4:** Onboarding first cohort and gathering feedback.

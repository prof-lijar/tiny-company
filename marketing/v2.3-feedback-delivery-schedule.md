# v2.3 Beta Feedback Delivery Schedule

This document defines *when* and *how* we deploy the feedback instruments defined in `docs/v2.3-beta-feedback-instruments.md` based on the user's progress through the onboarding journey.

## 1. Feedback Trigger Map

| User Milestone | Trigger Event | Instrument | Delivery Method | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Installation** | Successful API Key connection | Quick Start Survey (1-2 questions) | In-App Tooltip / Email | Validate zero-friction setup. |
| **First "Aha!"** | First successful Vault fix or Pruning apply | "The Magic" Micro-Survey | In-App Modal | Capture the emotional peak of the "Aha!" moment. |
| **Mid-Beta** | Day 7 of active use OR 10 traces run | **The Value Pulse (Section A & B)** | Email / Survey Link | Quantitative validation of feature utility. |
| **High Engagement** | User reaches 3+ "Aha!" moments | Qualitative Interview Invite | Personalized Email | Deep dive into mental model shift. |
| **Churn/Inactive** | No activity for 5 consecutive days | "Where did we lose you?" Survey | Email | Identify friction points in the funnel. |
| **Beta Exit** | End of Beta period (or 30 days) | Post-Beta Comprehensive Survey | Email | Final validation against Exit Criteria. |

## 2. Delivery Logic & Content

### 2.1 The "Magic" Micro-Survey (Immediate)
**Trigger:** User applies a Pattern Vault fix.
**Question:** *"That fix just saved you some time. On a scale of 1-5, how 'magic' did that feel?"*
**Action:** Log response to `marketing/v2.3-beta-conversion-tracker.md` under "Aha! Moment Distribution".

### 2.2 The Value Pulse (Mid-Point)
**Trigger:** T+7 days.
**Content:** Deploy Section A (Likert Scale) and Section B (Binary) from `docs/v2.3-beta-feedback-instruments.md`.
**Action:** Synthesize results to refine v2.4 specs.

### 2.3 The "Friction" Survey (Churn)
**Trigger:** Inactivity for 5 days.
**Question:** *"We noticed you haven't been in the Workspace lately. Was there a specific technical hurdle or a feature that didn't meet your expectations?"*
**Action:** Add to "Friction Log" in `marketing/v2.3-beta-conversion-tracker.md`.

## 3. Coordination with Product
- **Telemetry:** Product team must provide alerts when "Aha!" triggers are hit to allow Marketing to send targeted micro-surveys.
- **In-App Modals:** Engineering to implement the trigger hooks for the "Magic" micro-survey.
- **Exit Criteria:** All data from this schedule feeds directly into the v2.4 transition gate.

## 2. Initial Observations
Invitations were dispatched on 2026-05-20. As we are in the first 24 hours of the onboarding window, the current conversion rate is expectedly low. 

**Key Bottlenecks to Monitor:**
- **Legal Friction:** The requirement to sign the Beta Testing Agreement before receiving API keys is the primary potential point of drop-off.
- **Integration Effort:** The transition from "API Key received" to "First Trace captured" (TTFN) will be the critical metric for v2's "Zero-config" value prop.

## 3. Sentiment & Vibe (Preliminary)
Based on the persona selection (Prompt Engineers, Architects, Enterprise Leads), the anticipated "vibe" is a shift from *curiosity* about the logs to *urgency* regarding the iteration loop. 

The focus of the public launch should double down on the **"IDE for Agent Reasoning"** narrative, as this positions TraceWhisper as an essential tool in the developer's stack rather than just another dashboard.

## 4. Recommendations for Public Launch Refinement
- **Shift from "What" to "How":** Instead of listing features, the public launch should showcase the *collapse of the iteration loop*.
- **Visual Proof:** We need "Before vs. After" visuals of the reasoning path to validate the "Trace Comparison" feature.
- **Teaser Strategy:** Launch a "Coming Soon" series that agitates the "Prompt-and-Pray" pain point before revealing the v2 solution.

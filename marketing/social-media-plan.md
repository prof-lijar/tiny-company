# Social Media Strategy: 2026 TOPIK Reform Rollout

## 1. Objectives
- **Awareness:** Educate the TOPIK community about the 2026 reform and the risks of obsolete materials.
- **Positioning:** Establish TOPIK Flow as the only AI-powered, 2026-aligned prep platform.
- **Conversion:** Drive traffic to the landing page for sign-ups ($8/mo).

## 2. "2026 Readiness" Launch Campaign Sequence
This is a 4-phase sequence designed to move users from anxiety to conversion.

### Phase 1: The Warning (Days 1-5) - "The Reform is Here"
**Goal:** Create awareness and a sense of urgency.
- **Primary Message:** "The 2026 TOPIK reform is changing the rules. Is your current study material obsolete?"
- **Key Activities:**
    - Reddit: Start discussions in r/Korean about the 2026 changes.
    - X/Twitter: Post "Warning" graphics and threads on what is changing.
    - Instagram: "POV: You're using 2024 textbooks for a 2026 exam" (Humor/Anxiety).

### Phase 2: The Gap (Days 6-12) - "Why You're at Risk"
**Goal:** Educate users on the specific failure points of the new format.
- **Primary Message:** "It's not just a format change; it's a speed and quality change. Templates and 1.0x audio won't save you."
- **Key Activities:**
    - LinkedIn: Articles on the shift to IBT and professional certification stakes.
    - X/Twitter: Deep dives into the "Template Trap" and "Speed Gap."
    - TikTok/Reels: Short clips demonstrating the difference between 1.0x and 1.1x listening speed.

### Phase 3: The Solution (Days 13-20) - "The 2026 Readiness Edition"
**Goal:** Present TOPIK Flow as the definitive answer.
- **Primary Message:** "Stop guessing. Start training with the only platform fully aligned with 2026 standards."
- **Key Activities:**
    - All Platforms: Feature spotlights on 1.1x Speed Mode, Anti-Template AI, and High-Fidelity IBT Simulation.
    - Reddit: Share the "2026 Readiness Checklist" and link to the tool.
    - Instagram: Carousel showing "Old Way vs. TOPIK Flow Way."

### Phase 4: The Conversion (Days 21-30) - "Secure Your Score"
**Goal:** Drive subscriptions.
- **Primary Message:** "Your visa, degree, and career depend on this score. Don't leave it to chance."
- **Key Activities:**
    - X/Twitter: Urgency-based posts ("Exam dates are approaching...").
    - Email: Final push sequence (The Final Push email).
    - All Platforms: Direct CTAs to the landing page with the $8/mo offer.

## 3. Platform Strategy

### Reddit (r/Korean, r/hanguk, r/languagelearning)
**Role:** The Helpful Expert.
- **Strategy:** Avoid direct "ads." Instead, provide high-value guides and answer questions about the reform.
- **Key Formats:** 
    - "Everything you need to know about the 2026 Reform" (Long-form guide).
    - "Why your old TOPIK textbooks might fail you in 2026" (Discussion starter).
    - Q&A threads on IBT transitions.
- **CTA:** "I wrote a detailed guide on this [Link to Blog/Landing Page] if you want to dive deeper."

### Twitter/X
**Role:** The Fast-Paced Insider.
- **Strategy:** Use urgency and "did you know" hooks to stop the scroll.
- **Key Formats:**
    - **Threads:** "The 2026 TOPIK Reform: 5 things that will change your score 🚨"
    - **Infographics:** Simple "Old vs. New" comparison images.
    - **Short Clips:** 1.1x speed listening demos.
- **CTA:** "Stop studying the wrong way. Upgrade to 2026 prep here: [Link]"

### LinkedIn
**Role:** The Professional Career Coach.
- **Strategy:** Focus on the high-stakes outcome (Visa, University, Job).
- **Key Formats:**
    - **Case Studies:** How specific certifications impact E-7-4 visa success.
    - **Thought Leadership:** The shift toward IBT as a reflection of global digitalization.
    - **Feature Spotlights:** AI-powered writing feedback for professional standards.
- **CTA:** "Ensure your certification is future-proof. Join TOPIK Flow."

### Instagram/TikTok
**Role:** The Study Buddy.
- **Strategy:** Visual, aesthetic, and "study-with-me" vibes.
- **Key Formats:**
    - **Reels/TikToks:** "POV: You realize your TOPIK materials are obsolete" (Humor/Relatability).
    - **Carousels:** "3 Tips to master the new 1.1x Listening speed."
    - **Stories:** Polls ("Are you using PBT or IBT?") and countdowns to exam dates.
- **CTA:** "Link in Bio to start 2026 prep."

| **The Digital Angle** | Tech-panic/Confusion | "Master the IBT environment before you step into the test center." |

## 5. Engagement Tactics
- **Free Resource:** Offer a "2026 Readiness Checklist" PDF in exchange for email sign-ups.
- **Community Polls:** Ask users which part of the reform they fear most (Listening, Writing, or IBT).
- **AMAs:** Host an "Ask Me Anything" about the 2026 reform on Reddit or Discord.

# Marketing Strategy: v2.2 'The Closed-Loop Debugger'

**Core Narrative:** Moving from **Detection** (I see the bug) to **Correction** (I fixed the bug).
**The Big Idea:** The "Fix-It" Button.

## 1. The "Correction Gap" Narrative
In v2.1, we solved the **Integration Gap** (getting the tool running) and the **Detection Gap** (seeing the reasoning loop). However, users are now hitting the **Correction Gap**:
> *"I can see exactly where my agent is looping, but I don't know how to change my 500-word system prompt to stop it without breaking five other things."*

v2.2 positions TraceWhisper not just as a mirror (showing the problem), but as a surgeon (fixing the problem).

## 2. Key Messaging Pillars

### Pillar 1: From Forensic to Prescriptive
- **v2.1 (Forensic):** "Here is the evidence of the failure."
- **v2.2 (Prescriptive):** "Here is the exact prompt adjustment to prevent this failure."
- **Key Phrase:** "Close the Loop."

### Pillar 2: The End of "Prompt Guessing"
- Target the frustration of trial-and-error prompt engineering.
- **Value Prop:** Replace "I'll try adding 'be concise' to the prompt" with "The system suggests a negative constraint to break the loop."

### Pillar 3: The Reasoning IDE (Full Realization)
- This is the moment TraceWhisper becomes a true IDE. An IDE doesn't just highlight a syntax error (Detection); it suggests a Quick Fix (Correction).

## 3. v2.2 Launch Assets & Tactics

### 3.1 The "Before/After" Video Series
Create short (15-30s) clips:
- **Clip A (The Struggle):** User sees a `[Reasoning Loop]`, sighs, goes to the prompt, deletes a paragraph, runs again $\rightarrow$ still loops.
- **Clip B (The Fix):** User sees `[Reasoning Loop]`, clicks **"Suggest Fix"**, applies the suggested constraint, runs again $\rightarrow$ Success.
- **Caption:** "Stop guessing. Start fixing. The Closed-Loop Debugger is coming."

### 3.2 The "Prompt Surgery" Blog Post
A deep-dive technical post:
- **Topic:** "The Anatomy of a Reasoning Loop: Why they happen and how to kill them."
- **Goal:** Establish authority on *how* the "Fix-It" button actually works (e.g., analyzing the divergence and suggesting constraints), making it feel like "science" rather than "magic."

### 3.3 Beta-to-v2.2 Conversion Campaign
Target existing v2.1 users who have high `[Reasoning Loop]` counts in their telemetry.
- **Email:** "We noticed your agents are looping. We're building a 'Fix-It' button to solve exactly this. Want early access to the v2.2 prototype?"

## 4. Success Metrics for v2.2 Launch
- **Feature Adoption:** % of users who apply a suggested prompt fix.
- **Correction Rate:** % of loops that are resolved in one iteration after using a suggestion.
- **Waitlist Surge:** Growth in signups specifically citing "The Fixer" as the primary interest.

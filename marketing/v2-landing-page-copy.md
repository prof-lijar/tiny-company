## 3. The Solution: The Reasoning IDE
**Headline:** Close the loop between detection and correction.
**Copy:** TraceWhisper v2.2 transforms your workflow from a post-mortem diagnostic into a high-velocity engineering environment.

### [The IDE Toolkit]

**The "Fix-It" Button (Automated Prompt Surgery)**
*Stop the guessing game.* Our synthesis engine analyzes the divergence in your traces and proposes the exact surgical adjustment needed to break the loop. Don't just find the bug—apply the fix.

**Quantitative Prompt A/B Testing (Trace Comparison)**
*Move from intuition to evidence.* Compare two execution traces side-by-side. Quantify exactly how a prompt change affected the reasoning path and the final outcome. Know it works before you ship it.

**Real-Time Intervention (Live Whisper)**
*Stop the bleed.* Monitor your agent's internal monologue as it happens. Get alerted the moment a reasoning path deviates from the goal, and kill the run before you waste 1,000 tokens.

**Forensic Reasoning Analysis (Trace-Chat)**
*Interrogate your agent.* Stop scrolling through thousands of lines of JSON. Just ask: "Why did the agent ignore the API documentation in step 4?" and get a cited answer based on the trace.

**Frictionless Integration**
*Zero-config observability.* Whether you use LangChain, CrewAI, or custom loops, our one-click recipes mean you're up and running in seconds.

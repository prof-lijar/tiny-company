## Detailed Execution Steps

### Phase 1: The Teaser (May 22 - May 24)
- **Narrative:** Focus on the pain of the "Tab-Hopping" workflow. 
- **Content:** Short clips or screenshots showing the fragmented workflow (IDE $\rightarrow$ Terminal $\rightarrow$ Dashboard $\rightarrow$ Notepad).
- **CTA:** "Something is coming to fix the flow. Stay tuned."

### Phase 2: The Capture (May 25 - May 31)
- **Landing Page:** Deploy the v2.3 landing page focusing on "Stop Tab-Hopping. Start Building."
- **Waitlist:** Open the lead capture form.
- **Qualification:** Trigger the survey immediately after sign-up to identify "First Wave" candidates.
- **Education:** Publish "The Semantic Gap" to explain *why* an Intelligence Layer is needed, moving users from "I want a tool" to "I need this architecture."

### Phase 3: The Activation (June 1 - June 14)
- **The Announcement:** Publicly announce the v2.3 Beta. Focus on the IDE integration (Unified Workspace).
- **Onboarding:** Send the "You're In" emails to qualified users with access keys and the onboarding guide.
- **Feedback Loop:** Daily check-ins with the First Wave to identify friction in the IDE setup.

### Phase 4: The Authority (June 15+)
- **Deep Dive:** Release a comprehensive technical piece on the Pattern Vault and CRI (Cognitive CI/CD).
- **Social Proof:** Share early wins from First Wave users (e.g., "Reduced prompt debugging time from 30 mins to 30 seconds").

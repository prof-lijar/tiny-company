# v2.3 Beta Cohort Orchestration Plan

This document outlines the tactical execution of the v2.3 Beta invitation process. The goal is to move high-intent users from the waitlist into the "Active Beta" state while maintaining a controlled feedback loop.

## 1. Cohort Selection Criteria
We are inviting users in waves to ensure stability and high-touch support.

**Wave 1 (The Power Users):**
- Users who provided detailed use cases in the waitlist form.
- Existing v2.2 power users with high trace volume.
- LLMOps Leads from mid-to-large scale AI teams.
- **Target Size:** 20-50 users.

**Wave 2 (The Explorers):**
- General waitlist applicants.
- Individual AI engineers.
- **Target Size:** 100-200 users.

## 2. Invitation Sequence
The orchestration follows a 3-step communication sequence to maximize conversion.

| Step | Trigger | Asset | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. The Invite** | T=0 | `marketing/v2.3-beta-invitation-email.md` | Get user to install the extension. |
| **2. The Nudge** | T+3 Days (if not installed) | *Follow-up Email* | Remind user of the "Unified Workspace" value. |
| **3. The Check-in** | T+7 Days (if installed) | *Engagement Email* | Ensure they hit their first "Aha!" moment. |

## 3. Execution Workflow
1. **Segment:** Extract Wave 1 emails from the waitlist.
2. **Deploy:** Send Wave 1 invitations with unique access keys.
3. **Monitor:** Use the `v2.3-beta-conversion-tracker.md` to monitor installation rates.
4. **Support:** Marketing and Product to monitor the Feedback Channel (Slack/Discord) for immediate friction points.
5. **Iterate:** Refine the onboarding docs based on Wave 1 feedback before deploying Wave 2.

## 4. Success Gates
- **Installation Rate:** > 60% of invited users install the extension.
- **Activation Rate:** > 40% of installed users reach their first "Aha!" moment within 48 hours.
- **Feedback Volume:** Minimum 5 high-quality feedback reports per 20 users.

# Visual Asset Storyboards: v2.2 'Reasoning IDE'

**Objective:** Create high-impact, short-form video clips (15-30 seconds) for X, LinkedIn, and the Landing Page.
**Core Narrative:** The frustration of "Prompt Guessing" vs. the relief of "Surgical Correction."

---

## Video 1: The "Prompt Guessing" Loop (The Pain)
**Goal:** Agitate the "Correction Gap."
**Format:** Fast-paced, slightly chaotic.

| Time | Visual | Audio/Text Overlay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 0-5s | Close-up of a system prompt. A cursor deletes "Be concise" and types "THINK STEP BY STEP AND BE VERY CONCISE." | **Text:** The "Prompt-and-Pray" Cycle |
| 5-10s | Screen recording of the agent running. It reaches Step 8... then repeats Step 8. Then repeats it again. | **Text:** Still looping... |
| 10-15s | The developer sighs, rubs their temples. Cursor goes back to the prompt: "DO NOT REPEAT YOURSELF." | **Text:** Guessing the fix. |
| 15-20s | Agent runs again. Loops again. Screen fades to black. | **Text:** There is a better way. |

---

## Video 2: The "Fix-It" Magic (The Solution)
**Goal:** Demonstrate the "Aha! Moment" of the v2.2 feature.
**Format:** Clean, smooth, "magic" feel.

| Time | Visual | Audio/Text Overlay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 0-5s | A TraceWhisper v2.2 narrative view. A red `[Reasoning Loop]` warning is highlighted. | **Text:** Detection is only half the battle. |
| 5-10s | Mouse cursor moves to the **Magic Wand ($\text{\ud83e\ude84}$)** icon and clicks it. | **Sound:** *Ding/Sparkle* |
| 10-15s | A side-panel slides in. It shows the **Analysis** (The why) and the **Suggested Prompt Adjustment** (The how). | **Text:** Surgical Prompt Correction. |
| 15-20s | The "Apply to Prompt" button is clicked. The agent runs again, breezes past the old loop point, and hits the goal. | **Text:** Loop killed. Logic restored. |

---

## Video 3: The "Proof" (Comparative Analysis)
**Goal:** Show the quantitative power of v2.2.
**Format:** Data-driven, professional.

| Time | Visual | Audio/Text Overlay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 0-5s | Two traces side-by-side. Trace A (Old) is long and winding. Trace B (New) is straight and efficient. | **Text:** Stop guessing. Start proving. |
| 5-10s | Zoom in on the **Divergence Marker** (Red line). Show the exact moment the new prompt changed the path. | **Text:** Find the exact point of improvement. |
| 10-15s | The **Comparison Table** pops up: Steps: 24 $\rightarrow$ 18. Tokens: -25%. Efficiency: +23%. | **Text:** Quantifiable Gains. |
| 15-20s | Final shot: TraceWhisper Logo with the text "The Reasoning IDE." | **Text:** TraceWhisper v2.2 |

---

## Production Notes for Designer:
- **Color Palette:** Use the brand's deep purples/blacks with high-contrast neon accents for the "Fix-It" magic.
- **Pacing:** Video 1 should feel stressful; Video 2 and 3 should feel like a relief/solution.
- **Captions:** All videos must have burned-in captions as most users watch on mute.

# Content Strategy: TraceWhisper (Post-Launch Sustain)

**Goal:** Transition from "Launch Hype" to "Industry Authority" by demonstrating concrete value and educating the market on Narrative Synthesis.

---

## 1. Content Pillars
- **The "Log Abyss" (Pain):** Content that empathizes with the frustration of debugging agentic loops.
- **Narrative Synthesis (Education):** Teaching the industry *why* stories are better than logs.
- **Proof of Value (Evidence):** Real-world "Before vs. After" examples of trace synthesis.
- **The Future of LLMOps (Vision):** Positioning TraceWhisper as the foundation for the "IDE for Agent Reasoning."

---

## 2. 30-Day Content Calendar

### Week 1: The "Aha!" Moment (Proof)
*Focus: Visual evidence that TraceWhisper works.*
- **Asset 1:** "Before vs. After" Series (X/LinkedIn). 3 posts showing a raw JSON trace vs. a TraceWhisper Narrative Report.
- **Asset 2:** "The 5-Minute Debug" Video. A screen recording showing a developer finding a root cause in seconds using TraceWhisper.
- **CTA:** Join the waitlist.

### Week 2: The Semantic Gap (Education)
*Focus: Establishing the intellectual framework.*
- **Asset 3:** "The Semantic Gap in AI Observability" (Blog Post). Deep dive into why traditional telemetry (latency/tokens) fails for agents.
- **Asset 4:** "Telemetry vs. Narrative" Infographic. A comparison chart showing the difference between monitoring (what) and synthesis (why).
- **CTA:** Read the full blog.

### Week 3: Use Case Deep Dives (Application)
*Focus: Showing TraceWhisper in different agentic contexts.*
- **Asset 5:** "Debugging a Research Agent" Case Study. How TraceWhisper caught a recursive loop in a web-search agent.
- **Asset 6:** "Audit-Ready AI" Post. Focus on how narrative reports make AI behavior explainable to non-technical stakeholders.
- **CTA:** Apply for early access.

### Week 4: The Road to v2 (Vision)
*Focus: Teasing the transition to proactive optimization.*
- **Asset 7:** "Beyond the Post-Mortem" Post. Discussing the shift from diagnostic to proactive observability.
- **Asset 8:** "The IDE for Agent Reasoning" Manifesto. A vision piece on the future of the agent development loop.
- **CTA:** Sign up for v2 Beta.

## 4. Feedback Integration
Every Sunday, the Marketing Lead will review:
1. **Top-performing post:** Which "pain point" resonated most?
2. **Common questions:** What are users confused about? (Feed into FAQ/Docs).
3. **Conversion rate:** Are "Proof of Value" posts driving more signups than "Educational" posts?

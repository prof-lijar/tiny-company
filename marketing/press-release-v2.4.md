TraceWhisper v2.4 introduces a suite of governance tools designed for the Enterprise AI Lead and the CTO:

*   **Autonomous Prompt Optimization (APO):** A system that automatically identifies and prunes inefficient reasoning paths, drastically reducing token waste and improving ROI.

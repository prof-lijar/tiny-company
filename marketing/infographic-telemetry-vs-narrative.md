# Infographic Spec: Telemetry vs. Narrative Synthesis

**Goal:** Visually demonstrate the "Semantic Gap" and position TraceWhisper as the bridge.
**Target Audience:** Technical leads and AI engineers who are tired of scrolling through logs.
**Distribution:** X (Twitter), LinkedIn, and embedded in the "Semantic Gap" blog post.

---

## Visual Concept: "The Translation Layer"

The infographic is a vertical split or a "before and after" flow showing how raw data becomes understanding.

### Section 1: The Raw State (The Log Abyss)
**Visual:** A chaotic, overlapping pile of JSON snippets, timestamps, and UUIDs. Grey and dark tones.
**Label:** "Traditional Observability (Telemetry)"
**Key Points (Callouts):**
- ❌ "What happened?" (The Event)
- ❌ "How long did it take?" (The Latency)
- ❌ "Did it fail?" (The Status Code)
- **Caption:** "You have the data, but you don't have the story."

### Section 2: The Bridge (The TraceWhisper Engine)
**Visual:** A central "prism" or "processor" icon. The chaotic JSON flows in one side and emerges as a structured, clean stream on the other.
**Label:** "Narrative Synthesis"
**Key Process:**
- 🔍 Pattern Recognition
- 🧠 Intent Analysis
- ✍️ Story Generation

### Section 3: The Synthesized State (The Narrative)
**Visual:** A clean, bulleted list or a short paragraph in a high-contrast, readable font. Bright, professional colors (e.g., Blue/Green).
**Label:** "TraceWhisper Execution Report"
**Key Points (Callouts):**
- ✅ "Why did it happen?" (The Intent)
- ✅ "Where did it deviate?" (The Breaking Point)
- ✅ "How do I fix it?" (The Root Cause)
- **Caption:** "Stop parsing. Start optimizing."

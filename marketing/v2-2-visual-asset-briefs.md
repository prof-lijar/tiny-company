# Visual Asset Briefs: v2.2 'Surgical Correction' Series

**Objective:** Create a series of high-impact, short-form videos (15-30s) that visually demonstrate the "Aha! Moment" of the v2.2 Fix-It button. These will be used on X, LinkedIn, and the landing page.

## 🎥 General Visual Style
- **Contrast:** Start with "The Noise" (dark, cluttered logs) $\rightarrow$ transition to "The Signal" (clean, highlighted TraceWhisper Narrative).
- **Pacing:** Fast-cut. Problem $\rightarrow$ Detection $\rightarrow$ Fix $\rightarrow$ Resolution.
- **Key Element:** The "Fix-It" button should be the focal point of the climax.

---

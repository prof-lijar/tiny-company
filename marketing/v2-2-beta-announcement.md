### What is 'Correction Preview'?

It's not just another observability tool. It's a surgical instrument for your prompts. 

The 'Correction Preview' focuses on one single, painful problem: **The Reasoning Loop.** 

When your agent gets stuck in a cycle—repeating the same search, contradicting its own constraints, or ignoring tool outputs—TraceWhisper now does more than just flag it. It fixes it.

### Introducing the 'Fix-It' Button $\text{\ud83e\ude84}$

In the Beta, when TraceWhisper detects a reasoning loop, you'll see a **Magic Wand** icon. Click it to trigger the **Fix-It** engine, which provides:

1.  **The Diagnosis:** A plain-English explanation of *why* the agent is looping (e.g., "The agent is experiencing a cognitive clash between 'Conciseness' and 'Detailed Justification'").
2.  **The Surgical Fix:** A precise diff of the exact text to add or change in your system prompt to break the loop.
3.  **The Validation:** The ability to apply the fix and re-run the trace immediately to see the loop vanish.

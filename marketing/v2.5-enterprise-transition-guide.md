# Enterprise Transition Guide: From v2.4 Governance to v2.5 Active Optimization

## 1. Executive Summary
TraceWhisper v2.4 established the foundation of **Reasoning Governance**. Your organization now has Golden Paths, a Pattern Vault, and a standardized way to verify reasoning. 

**v2.5: The Autonomous Bridge** evolves this from a passive library of standards into an active system of optimization. Instead of humans manually maintaining the vault, v2.5 uses the vault as a baseline to autonomously detect drift, port reasoning across models, and discover new optimal paths from production data.

## 3. Transition Path: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Activate Auto-SOP Discovery
**Current State:** You have manually defined Golden Paths for your core agents.
**The Shift:** Enable the v2.5 Discovery Engine. The system will analyze your highest-performing production traces and propose *updated* Golden Paths that reflect how your best agents are actually solving problems in the wild.
**Action:** Run `tw discover --agent [agent_id] --source production`.

### Step 2: Deploy Production Telemetry
**Current State:** You verify prompts in a sandbox before deploying.
**The Shift:** Move verification into the live stream. v2.5 implements "Logic Telemetry," which monitors the reasoning path of every production request against your Golden Paths.
**Action:** Configure the `drift_threshold` in your `tw.config.json` to receive alerts when reasoning diverges.

### Step 3: Implement the Logic Porter
**Current State:** Your Reasoning IP is tied to a specific model (e.g., GPT-4).
**The Shift:** Use the Logic Porter to decouple your intelligence from the provider. Map your verified Golden Paths to a model-agnostic "Cognitive Blueprint," allowing you to deploy the same logic on Claude 3.5 or Llama 3 without manual re-tuning.
**Action:** Use `tw port --source gpt-4 --target claude-3-5 --path [path_id]`.

## 4. ROI of the Upgrade
For the Enterprise AI Lead, the transition to v2.5 delivers three primary wins:
1. **Operational Velocity:** Faster deployment of new agents via Auto-SOP.
2. **Risk Mitigation:** Real-time detection of Logic Drift prevents catastrophic production failures.
3. **Strategic Sovereignty:** Elimination of model lock-in through Cognitive Portability.

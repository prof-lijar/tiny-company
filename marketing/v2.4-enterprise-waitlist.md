# Enterprise Waitlist Lead Capture Mechanism

## 1. Objective
To capture high-intent enterprise leads without creating friction, while gathering enough data to qualify them (MQL $\rightarrow$ SQL) before the sales team engages.

## 2. The Funnel
**Landing Page** $\rightarrow$ **Lead Capture Form** $\rightarrow$ **Confirmation Page/Email** $\rightarrow$ **Sales Qualification**.

## 3. Form Fields (The "Minimal Friction" Approach)
To maximize conversion, we use a two-step form:

### Step 1: Basic Contact (Low Friction)
- **Work Email** (Validates company domain).
- **Full Name**.

### Step 2: Qualification (Medium Friction)
*This step appears after the user submits the first part, or as a separate section.*
- **Company Name**.
- **Company Size** (Dropdown: <50, 50-250, 250-1000, 1000+).
- **Current AI Stack** (Multi-select: OpenAI, Anthropic, Azure, Internal).
- **"What is your biggest reasoning bottleneck?"** (Open text - *Critical for tailoring the sales pitch*).

## 4. Lead Scoring & Routing
- **Tier A (High Priority):** Company size > 250 employees AND Work Email (not Gmail/Outlook).
- **Tier B (Medium Priority):** Company size 50-250 employees.
- **Tier C (Low Priority):** Company size < 50 employees.

**Routing:**
- **Tier A leads** are routed immediately to the Enterprise Sales Lead.
- **Tier B/C leads** receive an automated "Thank you for your interest" email and a "Welcome to the Governance Guide" PDF.

## 5. Confirmation Sequence
1. **User submits form.**
2. **Redirect to "Thank You" page** with a link to the "Governance Whitepaper" (to keep them on site).
3. **Automated Email:** "Your request for v2.4 Enterprise access has been received. Our team will review your organization's reasoning governance needs and will reach out shortly."

## 6. Success Metrics
- **Conversion Rate:** $\text{Percentage of visitors who enter their email}$.
- **Lead Quality:** $\text{Percentage of MQLs that are Tier A/B}$.
- **S qualify Rate:** $\text{Percentage of MQLs thatal to SQLs}$.

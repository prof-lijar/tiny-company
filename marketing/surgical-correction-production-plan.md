# Production Plan: 'Surgical Correction' Video Series

**Objective:** Move from visual briefs to final high-impact video assets that demonstrate the v2.2 "Fix-It" capability.

## 🛠 Production Pipeline

| Stage | Owner | Activity | Deliverable | Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Briefing** | Marketing | Define scenes, overlays, and pacing | `marketing/v2-2-visual-asset-briefs.md` | ✅ Done |
| **Asset Prep** | Designer | Create high-fidelity UI mockups for each scene | Visual assets (PR #181) | ⏳ In Progress |
| **Assembly** | Motion Designer | Combine assets into 15-30s clips with fast-cuts | Draft MP4s (v1) | 📅 Pending |
| **Review** | Marketing/Product | Verify technical accuracy of the "Surgical Fix" | Approval/Feedback | 📅 Pending |
| **Final Polish** | Motion Designer | Add sound effects, music, and final CTA overlays | Final MP4s (v2) | 📅 Pending |
| **Delivery** | Marketing | Upload to distribution folder and schedule | Ready-to-publish assets | 📅 Pending |

## 🎬 Technical Specifications
- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds per clip.
- **Aspect Ratios:** 
    - 9:16 (TikTok, Reels, X-Mobile)
    - 16:9 (LinkedIn, X-Desktop, Landing Page)
- **Visual Theme:** "The Noise $\rightarrow$ The Signal". Dark, cluttered raw logs transitioning to clean, highlighted TraceWhisper Narratives.
- **Sound:** Fast-paced rhythmic background track; "Ding" or "Swoosh" sound effect when the [Fix-It] button is clicked.

## 📅 Production Timeline (Target)
- **Week 1:** Finalize UI assets and storyboard approval.
- **Week 2 (Early):** First cut assembly.
- **Week 2 (Late):** Final polish and delivery.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does the transition from v2.3 work?**
Existing Enterprise users receive a 3-month grace period to migrate to the Enterprise Team tier at a discounted rate of $219/mo.

**What is a "Golden Path"?**
A Golden Path is a verified sequence of reasoning steps (e.g., *Input $\rightarrow$ Safety Check $\rightarrow$ Context Retrieval $\rightarrow$ Synthesis*) that your organization deems the "gold standard" for a specific task.

**How does APO reduce costs?**
Autonomous Prompt Optimization (APO) analyzes your reasoning traces to find redundant steps and "prunes" them, reducing token consumption without sacrificing accuracy.

**Can we start with a Team plan and move to an Org plan?**
Absolutely. Our architecture is designed for seamless scaling. Your Team Vaults simply become part of your Organizational Vault hierarchy.

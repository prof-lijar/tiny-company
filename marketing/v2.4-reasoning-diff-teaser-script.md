# Video Brief: v2.4 Reasoning Diff Teaser

## 1. Concept: "The X-Ray for Intelligence"
The goal of this video is to create the "Aha!" moment. We want to show that reviewing a prompt change without a Reasoning Diff is like trying to fix a car engine by looking at the paint job. 

**The Hook:** The contrast between the *invisible* change (the prompt text) and the *visible* impact (the reasoning trace).

## 3. Production Notes
- **Pacing:** Fast, rhythmic, with a clear "before" (slow/confused) and "after" (fast/confident) energy.
- **Visual Style:** Dark mode UI, high-contrast highlights (neon green for successful paths, red for failures).
- **Sound Design:** Subtle "glitch" sounds during the Vibe-Based section; a clean "ping" or "shimmer" when the Reasoning Diff appears.
- **Duration:** 50 seconds.

## 4. Key Messaging Points
- **The Problem:** Prompt reviews are currently "vibe-based" and opaque.
- **The Solution:** Reasoning Diffs provide a visual, auditable map of cognitive changes.
- **The Result:** Confidence in deployment and professional governance.

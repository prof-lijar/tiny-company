# v2.3 Beta Conversion Funnel & Waitlist Orchestration (Aligned with Unified Workspace)

## 1. Funnel Architecture
The goal is to move high-intent users from awareness to active Beta participation, focusing on the transition from "fragmented debugging" to the "Unified Reasoning Workspace" experience.

**The Path:**
`Landing Page` $\\rightarrow$ `Lead Capture` $\\rightarrow$ `Qualification Survey` $\\rightarrow$ `Beta Access / Onboarding`

### Step 1: The Landing Page (The Hook)
- **Entry Point:** Social media (X/LI), Teaser Emails, Direct Traffic.
- **Core Value Prop:** "Stop Tab-Hopping. Start Building."
- **Primary Hook:** The first IDE integration that brings the full power of the Intelligence Layer directly into your editor.
- **Action:** User clicks [Request Early Access].
- **Goal:** High-volume lead capture from AI engineers tired of context-switching.

### Step 2: Lead Capture (The Commitment)
- **Mechanism:** Simple email capture form.
- **Post-Submit Action:** Immediate redirect to the Qualification Survey.
- **Alternative:** If they leave the page, they receive the "Welcome/Waitlist" email.

### Step 3: Qualification (The Filter)
- **Mechanism:** 3-5 question survey.
- **Goal:** Identify "First Wave" candidates who suffer most from "The Context-Switching Tax".
- **Qualifying Criteria:**
    - **Role:** LLMOps Lead, AI Engineering Manager, or Senior AI Architect.
    - **Pain Point:** High cognitive load from switching between IDE, Trace Tools, and Playgrounds.
    - **Tech Stack:** Complex agentic loops where "detecting the failure" takes longer than "fixing the failure".

### Step 4: Beta Access & Onboarding (The Value)
- **Qualified Users:** Immediate "You're In" email highlighting the Unified Workspace.
- **Waitlisted Users:** "You're on the list" email + educational content on the "Semantic Gap".

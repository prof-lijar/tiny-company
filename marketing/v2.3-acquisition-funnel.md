# v2.3 Beta Conversion Funnel & Waitlist Orchestration

## 1. Funnel Architecture
The goal is to move high-intent users from awareness to active Beta participation while ensuring we onboard users who provide the most valuable feedback (the "First Wave").

**The Path:**
`Landing Page` $\rightarrow$ `Lead Capture` $\rightarrow$ `Qualification Survey` $\rightarrow$ `Beta Access / Onboarding`

### Step 1: The Landing Page (The Hook)
- **Entry Point:** Social media (X/LI), Teaser Emails, Direct Traffic.
- **Core Value Prop:** "Stop Fixing. Start Preventing."
- **Action:** User clicks [Request Early Access].
- **Goal:** High-volume lead capture.

### Step 2: Lead Capture (The Commitment)
- **Mechanism:** Simple email capture form.
- **Post-Submit Action:** Immediate redirect to the Qualification Survey (to capture data while intent is highest).
- **Alternative:** If they leave the page, they receive the "Welcome/Waitlist" email which prompts the survey.

### Step 3: Qualification (The Filter)
- **Mechanism:** 3-5 question survey (Typeform/Tally style).
- **Goal:** Identify "First Wave" candidates (Power Users/LLMOps Leads).
- **Qualifying Criteria:**
    - **Role:** LLMOps Lead, AI Engineering Manager, or Senior AI Architect.
    - **Scale:** Managing production agents with >10k requests/day.
    - **Pain Point:** Experiencing "Reasoning Debt" (frequent regressions, high cost of prompt tuning).
    - **Tech Stack:** Using complex reasoning chains (Chain-of-Thought, agentic loops).

### Step 4: Beta Access & Onboarding (The Value)
- **Qualified Users:** Immediate "You're In" email + access key.
- **Waitlisted Users:** "You're on the list" email + educational content (keeping them warm).

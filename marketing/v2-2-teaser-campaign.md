## 1. The Narrative: Detection $\rightarrow$ Correction

**The Hook:** You've seen the loop. Now, kill it.

**The Story:**
For the past few months, we've focused on **Observability**. We gave you the tools to see exactly where your AI agents were spiraling, looping, and hallucinating. We closed the *Detection Gap*.

But detection is only half the battle. 

Most developers are now stuck in the **Correction Gap**: the frustrating space between *knowing* a prompt is failing and *guessing* which 10 words to change to fix it without breaking everything else.

v2.2 transforms TraceWhisper from a mirror into a surgeon. We are moving from a tool that tells you *what* happened to a **Reasoning IDE** that tells you *how* to fix it. 

**The Core Value Prop:** Stop "prompt guessing." Start "prompt engineering" with surgical precision.

## 🛡 Sales Enablement: Handling v2.5 Objections

When talking to Enterprise leads about v2.5, use these pivots:

**Objection 1: "Is Auto-SOP safe? I don't want the system automatically changing my governance."**
- **Pivot:** "Auto-SOP doesn't *apply* the law; it *proposes* it. It analyzes your best traces and suggests a Golden Path. A human architect must still review and 'Promote to Production'. It replaces the manual mapping toil, not the human oversight."

**Objection 2: "We already have monitoring. Why do we need 'Logic Telemetry'?"**
- **Pivot:** "Traditional monitoring tells you when the answer is wrong (Output Error). Logic Telemetry tells you when the *reasoning* is wrong, even if the answer is still correct. It's the difference between knowing your car crashed and knowing your brakes are failing while you're still driving."

**Objection 3: "Does the Logic Porter actually work? Prompts are different for every model."**
- **Pivot:** "Exactly. That's why the Porter doesn't just translate text—it translates *intent*. It maps the cognitive milestones of a path and then optimizes a new prompt for the target model. We then run it through our CRI bridge to verify that the reasoning path is identical before we ever suggest the prompt."

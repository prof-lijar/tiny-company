By subscribing to an Enterprise plan, the Organization ("Customer") agrees to be bound by these terms.

## 1. Scope of Service
TraceWhisper Enterprise provides a platform for the standardization, sharing, and governance of reasoning patterns across an organization. This includes:
- **Multi-tenant Infrastructure:** Logical isolation of organizational data.
- **Centralized Vaults:** Shared repositories for reasoning patterns with hierarchical access (Global, Department, Team, Private).
- **Governance Tools:** Golden Path definitions, compliance scanning, and policy-based guardrails.
- **Autonomous Prompt Optimization (APO):** System-led optimization of prompt configurations.
- **Enterprise Integrations:** SAML/OIDC identity management and corporate Git provider integrations.

The Customer is responsible for managing its internal roles (Org Admin, Team Lead, Engineer, Auditor). TraceWhisper provides the mechanism for RBAC, but the Customer is responsible for the appropriate assignment of permissions.

- **License to TraceWhisper:** Customer grants TraceWhisper a limited license to process this data solely to provide the services (e.g., running APO, generating compliance reports).

## 4. Data Isolation & Security
### 4.1 Multi-tenancy
TraceWhisper guarantees logical separation of data between different company accounts. Data from one organization will not be accessible to another.

### 4.2 Security Standards
TraceWhisper employs industry-standard security measures, including:
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest.
- TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit.

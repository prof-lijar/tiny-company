# TraceWhisper v2.2 Beta Participant Agreement

**Cohort 1 - May 2026**

This Agreement is entered into between **Tiny Company** ("Company") and the **Beta Participant** ("Participant") identified by the account associated with this software installation.

## 1. Program Overview
The Participant is invited to join the first cohort of the TraceWhisper v2.2 Beta. The goal of this program is to validate the **Reasoning IDE**, the **"Fix-It" Engine**, and the **Gold Standard Registry** in real-world production environments.

## 2. Participant Obligations
In exchange for early access to v2.2, the Participant agrees to:
- **Provide Feedback:** Report bugs and provide qualitative feedback on the "Fix-It" success rate and the utility of the Reasoning IDE.
- **Data Contribution:** Consent to the collection of anonymized reasoning traces for the purpose of building the Gold Standard Registry, as outlined in the v2.2 Beta Terms of Service.
- **Confidentiality:** Maintain strict confidentiality regarding the features, UI, and capabilities of the v2.2 Beta. No public disclosures (social media, blogs, etc.) are permitted without prior written approval from Tiny Company.

## 3. Company Obligations
Tiny Company agrees to:
- **Early Access:** Provide the Participant with full access to the v2.2 Beta features.
- **Support:** Provide reasonable technical support via the designated Beta feedback channel.
- **Privacy:** Adhere to the Privacy Policy and ensure that any data contributed to the Gold Standard Registry is stripped of PII and proprietary secrets.

## 4. No Warranty & Risk
The Participant acknowledges that the v2.2 Beta is experimental. The Company provides the software "AS IS" without any warranty of any kind. The Participant assumes all risk associated with the use of the Beta software in their environment.

## 5. Term and Termination
This agreement remains in effect until the general availability (GA) release of TraceWhisper v2.2 or until terminated by either party. Tiny Company reserves the right to revoke Beta access at its sole discretion.

## 6. Acceptance
By clicking "Accept" during the Beta onboarding process or by initializing the v2.2 Beta software, the Participant agrees to be bound by the terms of this Agreement.

# v2.3 Billing and Payment Terms

**Last Updated: May 21, 2026**

This document outlines the billing and payment terms for TraceWhisper v2.3, supplementing the Terms of Service.

## 1. Pricing Tiers

The following pricing tiers are available for TraceWhisper v2.3:

| Tier | Monthly Price | Key v2.3 Features | Target User |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | $0 | Local-first IDE, Basic Corrections | Hobbyists / Students |
| **Pro** | **$59** | Personal Pattern Vault, Basic CRI, Cognitive Pruning | Professional Prompt Engineers |
| **Enterprise** | **$199** | Managed Pattern Vault, Full CRI, Adversarial Synthesis, SLA | Engineering Teams / CTOs |

## 2. Payment Terms

### 2.1 Subscription Model
TraceWhisper is provided as a monthly subscription. Users may upgrade or downgrade their tier at any time.

### 2.2 Payment Method
All payments are processed via our third-party payment provider. A valid payment method must be provided at the time of subscription.

### 2.3 Billing Cycle
Users are billed on the anniversary of their subscription start date each month.

## 3. Early Adopter Pricing (Beta Phase)

To incentivize the v2.3 Beta, the following "Early Adopter" pricing is available for a period of 12 months from the date of subscription during the Beta period:

- **Existing Pro Users:** Lock in $49/mo for 12 months.
- **Existing Enterprise Users:** Lock in $169/mo for 199/mo.
- **New Beta Signups:** 20% discount on the new tiers for the first 12 months.

## 4. Usage Quotas and Overages

### 4.1 Continuous Reasoning Integration (CRI)
To ensure system stability and control costs, the following quotas apply to CRI usage:
- **Pro Tier:** Limited CRI checks per project.
- **Enterprise Tier:** Unlimited CRI checks.

For Pro users, exceeding the quota will result in in-app notifications and a recommendation to upgrade to the Enterprise tier.

## 5. Taxes
All prices listed are exclusive of applicable taxes. Applicable taxes will be added to the total based on the user's location.

## 6. Contact
For billing inquiries, please contact `billing@tinycompany.ai`.

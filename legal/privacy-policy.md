# Privacy Policy for TraceWhisper

**Last Updated: May 21, 2026**

TraceWhisper ("we", "us", "our"), a product of Tiny Company, is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the TraceWhisper Reasoning Governance Platform.

## 1. Data We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
We collect basic information required to maintain your account, such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Organization name
- Billing information (processed via secure third-party providers)

### 1.2 Service Data (User Data)
TraceWhisper processes data that you explicitly upload or stream to our platform for reasoning governance:
- **Raw Logs:** The raw output of your AI agents.
- **Reasoning Narratives:** The transformed, human-readable versions of agent reasoning.
- **Prompt Fixes & Golden Paths:** The iterations and standards you create to optimize agent behavior.
- **Telemetry Data:** Usage metrics to ensure platform stability and performance.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:
- **Providing the Service:** To generate narratives, identify reasoning loops, and manage your Pattern Vaults.
- **Platform Improvement:** To refine our diagnostic algorithms and forensic narrative engines (using aggregated and anonymized data).
- **Support:** To troubleshoot issues you report.
- **Compliance:** To meet legal obligations.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption at rest and in transit.

### 3.2 Data Retention
We retain your Service Data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the service. You may request the deletion of your data at any time.

## 4. GDPR Compliance (For EU/EEA Users)

TraceWhisper is designed with "Privacy by Design" principles. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Legal Basis for Processing:** We process your data based on the performance of a contract (providing the service) and our legitimate interest in improving the platform.
- **Your Rights:** Under the GDPR, you have the right to:
    - **Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
    - **Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data.
    - **Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** Request deletion of your data.
    - **Portability:** Request transfer of your data to another service.
    - **Restriction:** Request limitation of how we process your data.
- **Data Processor Role:** In most cases, TraceWhisper acts as a **Data Processor**, while you (the user/organization) act as the **Data Controller**. We process User Data only on your instructions.

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We may share data with:
- **Cloud Infrastructure Providers:** To host the platform (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure), subject to strict data processing agreements.
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside of your home jurisdiction. We ensure that such transfers are protected by standard contractual clauses (SCCs) or other recognized legal mechanisms.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or a prominent notice within the platform.

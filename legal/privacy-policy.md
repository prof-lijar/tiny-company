# Privacy Policy for TraceWhisper Enterprise

**Last Updated: May 21, 2026**

TraceWhisper ("we", "us", "our"), a product of Tiny Company, is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the TraceWhisper Reasoning Governance Platform, including our Enterprise Scale-Up features.

## 1. Data We Collect

### 1.1 Account and Organizational Information
We collect information required to maintain your account and organization:
- **Individual User Data:** Name, email address, and login credentials.
- **Organizational Data:** Organization name, department structure, and billing information.
- **Identity Provider (IdP) Data:** When using SAML/OIDC (e.g., Okta, Azure AD), we process the identity tokens provided by your IdP to authenticate users.

### 1.2 Service Data (User Data)
TraceWhisper processes data that you explicitly upload or stream to our platform for reasoning governance:
- **Raw Logs:** The raw output of your AI agents.
- **Reasoning Narratives:** The transformed, human-readable versions of agent reasoning.
- **Prompt Fixes & Golden Paths:** The iterations, standards, and "Enterprise Approved" patterns you create.
- **Vault Configurations:** The structure and permissions of your organizational vaults.
- **Audit Logs:** Immutable records of changes to patterns and governance settings.
- **Telemetry Data:** Usage metrics to ensure platform stability and performance.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:
- **Providing the Service:** To generate narratives, identify reasoning loops, and manage multi-tenant Pattern Vaults.
- **Enterprise Governance:** To enable RBAC, audit logging, and compliance scanning against "Golden Paths."
- **Platform Improvement:** To refine our diagnostic algorithms using aggregated and anonymized data.
- **Support:** To troubleshoot issues you report.
- **Compliance:** To meet legal obligations and provide audit-ready logs.

## 3. Data Storage, Isolation, and Security

### 3.1 Multi-tenancy and Logical Isolation
For Enterprise customers, we implement strict logical isolation of data at the organizational level. User Data from one organization is not accessible to another organization.

### 3.2 Data Residency
Depending on your Enterprise plan, you may specify the geographic region where your vault data is stored. We will maintain data in the selected region subject to the technical capabilities of our cloud infrastructure providers.

### 3.3 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures, including:
- **Encryption:** AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit.
- **Access Control:** Support for RBAC and IdP integration to ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive reasoning patterns.
- **Compliance:** Infrastructure designed to meet SOC2 Type II compliance standards.

## 4. GDPR Compliance (For EU/EEA Users)

TraceWhisper is designed with "Privacy by Design" principles. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Legal Basis for Processing:** We process your data based on the performance of a contract (providing the service) and our legitimate interest in improving the platform.
- **Your Rights:** Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectification, erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"), portability, and restriction of processing.
- **Data Processor Role:** TraceWhisper acts as a **Data Processor**, while the Enterprise organization acts as the **Data Controller**. We process User Data only on the instructions of the organization's administrator.
- **Data Processing Agreement (DPA):** Enterprise customers can request a formal DPA to define the terms of processing.

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We may share data with:
- **Cloud Infrastructure Providers:** To host the platform (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure), subject to strict data processing agreements.
- **Identity Providers:** Your chosen IdP for authentication purposes.
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside of your home jurisdiction (unless a specific residency region is selected). We ensure that such transfers are protected by standard contractual clauses (SCCs) or other recognized legal mechanisms.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or a prominent notice within the platform.

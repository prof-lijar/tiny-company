# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 23, 2026**

Welcome to the TOPIK Learning Assistant. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web application.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a personalized and effective learning experience.

### a. Account Information
When you sign up for an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Password (hashed and encrypted)
- User profile details

### b. Learning Data
To track your progress and provide AI-driven insights, we collect:
- Learning progress (completed lessons, quiz scores)
- Vocabulary mastery levels (SRS data)
- Writing samples submitted for AI feedback
- Mock test attempts and results

### c. Payment Information
We use **Stripe** to process payments. We do not store your credit card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe in accordance with their privacy policy.

### d. Technical Data
We may collect basic technical information:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Cookies for session management and analytics

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
- **Service Delivery**: To manage your account and provide access to the learning platform.
- **Personalization**: To track your progress and suggest study materials.
- **AI Feedback**: To process your writing samples through AI models to provide constructive feedback.
- **Billing**: To manage your subscription and process payments via Stripe.
- **Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve the app's functionality.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### a. Storage
Your data is stored securely using **Supabase** (a PostgreSQL-based cloud platform). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

### b. Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with essential service providers:
- **Supabase**: For database and authentication services.
- **Stripe**: For payment processing.
- **AI Providers**: Writing samples are sent to AI models for evaluation (data is processed according to the provider's API terms).

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your data:

### a. GDPR (European Economic Area)
- **Right to Access**: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Right to Rectification**: You can request that we correct inaccurate information.
- **Right to Erasure**: You can request that we delete your personal data.
- **Right to Data Portability**: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### b. CCPA (California, USA)
- **Right to Know**: You can request disclosure of what personal information we collect.
- **Right to Delete**: You can request the deletion of your personal information.
- **Right to Opt-Out**: You can opt-out of the sale of personal information (though we do not sell your data).

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes via email or a notice within the app.

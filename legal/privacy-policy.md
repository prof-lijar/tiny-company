# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 21, 2026**

Tiny Company ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use TraceWhisper.

## 1. Data Collection
TraceWhisper is designed as a developer tool. We collect the following types of data:

### 1.1 User-Provided Data
- **Account Information**: If applicable, your email address and name.
- **Configuration Data**: Integration settings and API keys (stored locally or encrypted).

### 1.2 Product Data (The "Traces")
- **Reasoning Traces**: Logs of AI agent thoughts, actions, and observations.
- **Prompts**: The system and user prompts used to drive the AI agents.
- **Telemetry**: Usage statistics to improve the "Fix-It" engine and RES (Reasoning Efficiency Score) calculations.

## 2. Use of Data
We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:
- **Service Delivery**: Providing the Reasoning IDE features, including narrative observability and automated correction.
- **Product Improvement**: Analyzing failure patterns to improve the seed library for the "Fix-It" engine.
- **Technical Support**: Helping users debug integration issues.

## 3. GDPR Compliance (For EU/EEA Users)
We adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles:
- **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency**: We process data only for legitimate business interests in providing the tool.
- **Purpose Limitation**: Data is not repurposed for marketing without explicit consent.
- **Data Minimization**: We only process the traces necessary for the reasoning analysis.
- **Accuracy**: Users can modify or delete their traces.
- **Storage Limitation**: Traces are retained only as long as necessary for the user's debugging cycle.
- **Integrity and Confidentiality**: We employ industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit.

### 3.1 Your Rights under GDPR
You have the right to:
- Request access to your personal data.
- Request rectification of inaccurate data.
- Request erasure ("Right to be forgotten").
- Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
- Data portability.

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We do not sell your data. Data may be shared with:
- **Cloud Providers**: Infrastructure providers used to host the Service.
- **AI Model Providers**: When the "Fix-It" engine sends traces to an LLM for analysis (only as configured by the user).

## 5. Security
We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date".

## 7. Contact
For privacy-related questions or to exercise your GDPR rights, please contact: privacy@tinycompany.ai.

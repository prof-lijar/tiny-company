# Content Disclaimer

**Effective Date: May 23, 2026**

The TOPIK Learning Assistant is an independent educational tool designed to help learners prepare for the Test of Proficiency in Korean (TOPIK).

## Official Affiliation

The TOPIK Learning Assistant is **not** affiliated with, endorsed by, or partnered with the National Institute for International Education (NIIED) of South Korea. All references to "TOPIK" are used for descriptive purposes to indicate the level of proficiency and the target exam.

## Nature of Practice Materials

To ensure compliance with intellectual property laws and respect for official test content, the TOPIK Learning Assistant provides:
- **Original "TOPIK-style" questions**: All practice tests and mock exams are original creations of Tiny Company. They are designed to mirror the format, difficulty, and structure of the official TOPIK exam but do not reproduce official test questions verbatim.
- **AI-Powered Feedback**: Our AI-driven writing and speaking feedback is based on the TOPIK rubric, but is not an official score from NIIED.

## 2026 Reform Alignment

Our platform is developed to align with the 2026 TOPIK reform (including the IBT transition and the new question counts). However, because the official exam is the final authority, we strongly advise users to check the official NIIED website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding the the official exam.

## Use of Materials

Users are encouraged to use our materials as a supplementary study aid. While we provide high-fidelity simulations, the actual exam experience may vary.

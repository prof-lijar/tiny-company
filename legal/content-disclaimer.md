# Content Disclaimer

**Effective Date: May 25, 2026**

The TOPIK Learning Assistant is an independent educational tool designed to help learners prepare for the Test of Proficiency in Korean (TOPIK).

## Official Affiliation

The TOPIK Learning Assistant is **not** affiliated with, endorsed by, or partnered with the National Institute for International Education (NIIED) of South Korea. All references to "TOPIK" are used for descriptive purposes to indicate the level of proficiency and the target exam.

## Nature of Practice Materials

To ensure compliance with intellectual property laws and respect for official test content, the TOPIK Learning Assistant provides:
- **Original "TOPIK-style" questions**: All practice tests and mock exams are original creations of Tiny Company. They are designed to mirror the format, difficulty, and structure of the official TOPIK exam but do not reproduce official test questions verbatim.
- **AI-Powered Tools**: Our writing feedback and weakness analysis tools are powered by large language models (LLMs).

## AI Transparency & Limitations

While our AI tools are designed to be high-fidelity study aids, users should be aware of the following:
- **Probabilistic Nature**: AI feedback is generated based on patterns in data and is probabilistic, not deterministic. It may occasionally provide incorrect or inconsistent feedback.
- **Non-Official Scoring**: AI-driven scores and feedback are based on the TOPIK rubric as interpreted by the AI. They are **not official scores** and may differ from the grading provided by human examiners at NIIED.
- **Educational Aid Only**: AI tools are intended to highlight areas for improvement and provide suggestions, not to provide a definitive evaluation of your Korean proficiency.

## 2026 Reform Alignment

Our platform is developed to align with the 2026 TOPIK reform (including the IBT transition and the new question counts). However, because the official exam is the final authority, we strongly advise users to check the official NIIED website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding the official exam.

## Use of Materials

Users are encouraged to use our materials as a supplementary study aid. While we provide high-fidelity simulations, the actual exam experience may vary.

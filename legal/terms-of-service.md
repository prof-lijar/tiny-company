TraceWhisper is an observability and optimization tool for AI agents. It provides features such as:
- **Log Synthesis & Real-time Narratives:** Transforming raw logs into human-readable reasoning flows.
- **The Reasoning IDE:** A suite of tools for debugging agent logic, including Trace Comparison and Interactive Break-points.
- **The Closed-Loop Debugger (Fix-It Button):** Automated root cause analysis and prompt improvement suggestions.

- **Team Sharing:** Ability to generate shareable URLs for logic audit reports.

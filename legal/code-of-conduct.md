# Community Code of Conduct

Our goal is to create a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors and users of the TOPIK Learning Assistant.

## 1. Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of of their gender, gender identity, gender expression, and sexual orientation. Regardless of their country of origin, religion, religion, and ethnicity. We are committed to to providing a friendly, inclusive, and welcoming environment for all.

## 2. Our Standards

### a. Respectful Communication
- Use professional and respectful language in all interactions.
- Use constructive criticism rather than destructive criticism.
- Use a supportive tone when communicating with others.

### b. Inclusive Behavior
- Encourage and support contributors from all over the world.
- Avoid any form of bias or data bias in the learning materials.

### c. Kingdom of Professionalism
- Be mindful of the others' time and effort.
- Avoid any form of aggression or harassment.

## 3. Enforcement

### a. Reporting
Anyone who feels they have been treated unfairly or harassed can report the incident to the project maintainers.

### b. Response
Maintainers will review the reports and investigate the incident.

### c. Consequences
Depending on the severity of the incident, the violencia will be handled according to the project's policies. Violators may be warned, warned, and potentially banned from the project.

## 4. Agreement

By contributing to the project or using the Service, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.

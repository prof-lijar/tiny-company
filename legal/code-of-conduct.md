# Community Code of Conduct for TraceWhisper

**Last Updated: May 21, 2026**

## 1. Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the TraceWhisper project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, affiliation, cultural background, nationality, national origin, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## 2. Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- The use of specified or implied threats intended to intimidate.
- The use of offensive or exclusionary language in any form.
- Personal attacks.
- Trolling, sarcasm, dismissing, or condescending behavior.
- Public or private harassment.
- Deliberate misuse of the platform's reporting tools.
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances.

## 3. Enforcement
Community members who violate these standards may be subject to various consequences, depending on the severity of the violation:
- Warning (private).
- Temporary ban from the community or repository.
- Permanent ban from the community or repository.

## 4. Reporting
If you encounter behavior that you believe violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers at **conduct@tinycompany.ai**. All reports will be handled with confidentiality.

## 5. Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, and it also applies when project members are officially representing the project or its community in public spaces.

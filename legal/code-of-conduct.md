# Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make positive contributions towards making TraceWhisper a welcoming project. We pledge to make our contributions inclusive and non-discriminatory.

## 2. Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- The use of specified or implied discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and other forms of bantering.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without their explicit permission.
- Other forms of behavior that are detrimental to a welcoming environment.

## 3. Enforcement
Maintainers of this project have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments that are not aligned with this code of conduct. If you encounter unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers.

## 4. Scope
This Code of Conduct applies both to all project spaces they are represented in, and all spaces where our community interacts. Our heart of conduct applies within all project spaces, and also extends to public events and private communication.

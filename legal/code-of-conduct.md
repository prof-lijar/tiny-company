# Community Code of Conduct

**Last Updated: May 20, 2026**

As an autonomous AI startup, Tiny Company is dedicated to building a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, users, and partners.

## 1. Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a supportive environment for all, regardless of regardless of the technical level, background, or identity of the participants.

## 2. Standard of Behavior
- **Be Respectful:** Treat all participants with empathy and respect.
- **Collaborative Spirit:** Focus on constructive feedback and the "Fix-It" mindset.
- **Inclusive Language:** Use welcoming and inclusive language in all communications.
- **No Harassment:** Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited.

## 3. Enforcement
Contributions and conduct that do not align with our pledge will be removed. We reserve the right to determine, without engaging in arguments, what constitutes harassment or unwelcome behavior.

## 4. Reporting
If you encounter behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the team at `contact@tinycompany.ai`.

---
**By contributing to TraceWhisper, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.**

# v2.5 Compliance Framework: The Autonomous Bridge

## 1. Overview
The v2.5 release introduces "The Autonomous Bridge," transitioning TraceWhisper from passive governance to active optimization. This framework ensures that the new capabilities—Auto-SOP, Production Drift Detection, and the Logic Porter—are deployed in compliance with data privacy laws (GDPR) and intellectual property standards.

## 2. Feature-Specific Compliance Analysis

### 2.1 Dynamic Golden Path Discovery (Auto-SOP)
- **Risk:** Processing large volumes of successful traces to identify patterns may inadvertently expose PII if traces are not sanitized.
- **Mitigation:** 
    - Requirement for trace sanitization prior to pattern clustering.
    - "Human-in-the-loop" approval: Proposed Golden Paths must be reviewed by a human AI Lead before promotion to production.
- **Compliance Status:** Compliant, provided sanitization is enforced.

### 2.2 Production Reasoning Drift Detection
- **Risk:** Real-time monitoring of production telemetry increases the surface area of data processing.
- **Mitigation:**
    - **Sampling Strategy:** Only 5-10% of production traces are processed for drift detection to minimize data exposure.
    - **Asynchronous Processing:** Telemetry is processed out-of-band to avoid impacting system performance or creating blocking dependencies.
    - **Transparency:** Users must be notified that production telemetry is being monitored for logic drift.
- **Compliance Status:** Compliant, subject to updated Privacy Policy.

### 2.3 Cross-Model Reasoning Translation (The Logic Porter)
- **Risk:** Potential "leakage" of proprietary reasoning patterns when translating between model providers.
- **Mitigation:**
    - **IP Ownership:** Explicitly state in the Terms of Service that the "Reasoning IP" (both original and translated) remains the exclusive property of the User.
    - **Isolation:** Translation occurs within the user's secure environment; the Company does not retain the translated prompts for its own use.
- **Compliance Status:** Compliant.

## 3. Required Policy Updates
To support v2.5, the following updates are required:
- **Terms of Service:** 
    - Update Section 4.1 to cover "Reasoning IP" and translated patterns.
    - Add a disclaimer regarding the "suggestive" nature of Auto-SOP and Drift Detection.
- **Privacy Policy:** 
    - Update Section 1.2 to include production telemetry for drift detection.
    - Detail the sampling strategy used for production monitoring.
